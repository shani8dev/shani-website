Why Shanios?

Shanios is crafted to ensure your system stays fresh and unbreakable, even after major updates. Our key pillars:

  • Immutable Deployments: A read-only foundation prevents unwanted changes and preserves stability.
  • Blue/Green Strategy: Upgrade with zero downtime, and roll back instantly if needed.
  • Built-In Security: Every component is hardened, keeping vulnerabilities at bay.
  • Flatpak Integration: All user applications are provided as Flatpaks, simplifying updates and keeping apps isolated.
  • Lean & Simple: No fuss—just an OS that stays out of your way.

About Shanios

Shanios was born out of a vision to revolutionize how we handle system updates. With an immutable architecture at its core, Shanios ensures each deployment is validated, secure, and tamper-proof. Our approach to atomic updates and a blue/green deployment model guarantees you’ll never worry about a broken upgrade again.
